Why Plants Matter

Plants are essential to life on Earth. They produce oxygen, provide food, and create habitats for countless species.

Types of Plants

Flowering Plants

These plants produce flowers and seeds. Examples include roses, sunflowers, and tulips.

Ferns

Ferns are ancient plants that reproduce via spores and have lush green fronds.

Succulents

Succulents store water in their leaves and stems, making them drought-resistant.

Plant Care Tips

  • Water plants regularly but avoid overwatering.
  • Provide adequate sunlight based on plant type.
  • Use quality soil and fertilizers.
  • Prune dead or damaged leaves to encourage growth.
